About Us

Queen's University Belfast Fencing Club is open to everyone interested in trying out the Olympic sport of modern fencing.

Beginners are more than welcome. Equipment is provided and club training is suitable to people of all fitness levels, making it easy and inexpensive to try the sport. Our coaches provide comprehensive beginner sessions at the start of each academic year. Additional lessons can be arranged upon request.

We also work closely with our governing body, Northern Ireland Fencing, to provide high-level coaching to our experienced fencers across all three weapons. Our club members regularly take part in regional, national and international events. We field teams in all categories and spaces are always available for our new fencers to gain invaluable experience in a supportive friendly environment.
